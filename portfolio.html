Purpose: Bamazon is a CLI tool that has 3 different modes with different abilities. The modes are:
Customer. In customer mode, you can view all of the products for sale, including their prices and departments.
Manager. In manager mode, you can view all of the products available for sale, view products with low inventory, add inventory to any product, or add a new item.
