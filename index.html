About Me

Welcome to my portfolio site. Please navigate to my portfolio page to check out a few examples of my projects. I've included links to my LinkedIn page, and Github page below, as well.

To tell you a bit about myself, I have an education in biology, and went on to get my Master's degree at Columbia, specifically studying bat ecology. After spending some time learning how to run a business at a science-education startup, I pivoted and joined a fast moving eCommerce startup, which was quickly acquired by one of the largest retailers in our nation. There, as a buyer, I had a front-row seat to the competitive retail tech industry, which sparked my desire to become a web developer.

This unique mix of education and professional experience has given me the ability to think critically and approach complex problems from a variety of angles, while keeping the customer top of mind.

I hope you enjoy my portfolio, and I welcome any feedback you have.
